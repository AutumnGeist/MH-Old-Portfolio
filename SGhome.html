Herbal Medicine

Since the dawn of humankind, herbal medicine has been used to treat everything from common ailments and injuries, to serious disease. It is the oldest-known form of healing, and is still used today in modern medicine. It can be defined as the use of specific herbs for their medicinal or therapeutic value.

Why Use Herbal Medicine?

Herbal remedies can serve as an excellent solution for many common conditions, and can be just as effective as comparable pharmaceuticals, but without the potentially harmful side effects and drug interactions. If you feel a bit skeptical about using herbal medicine, you are not alone! Herbal medicine is not about rejecting sound scientific facts, and an educated Doctor's advice! Instead it is about using safe, evidence-based alternatives that is natural and noninvasive to your body. Most remedies has a very low risk of durg interaction or toxicity, and can greatly improve your health when used properly!

Top 10 Reasons To Try Medicinal Herbs

  1. Proven to be as effective as pharmaceuticals
  2. Fewer side effects and less toxicity
  3. Easily absorbed by the body
  4. Complement corresponding therapies
  5. Long history of healing
  6. More affordable option
  7. Treat more than just single symptoms
  8. Enhances emotional well-being
  9. Sustainable medicine used worldwide
  10. Provides a deeper connection to nature

Please Note!

Herbal medicine is not foolproof nor miraculous, and is not meant to be used as a replacement for the best that modern medicine has to offer! It can however, be used to complement and enhance convential therapies. When in doubt, please always consult with your Doctor or Herbal Practitioner.
